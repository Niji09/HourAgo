HourAgo Story

How often you have cared about author or source of the news article? Well... I read a ton of news articles and posts online but never found a source which have informed me about the author of news article before reading the article. You have to deep dive into source website without knowing. Sometime they don't even bother to mention author name inside news article and it is agonizing.

You can find news article more credible by knowing the source or author of news article. HourAgo aims to provide information such as news source, author, publication date and description about news article before you read the article.

HourAgo collect information from Google's NewsApi.org website. HourAgo also calculate data(in %) of news articles based upon availability of data collected from NewsApi.org

Why HourAgo?

HourAgo is trying to prevent reader from fake news and a great way to do it by providing more data about news article itself. News article is more credible when you know more about it.

HourAgo uses simple data analysis tools to calculate the data of news articles. HourAgo's Data Availability element does not tell you the qualitiy of news article. Data Availability is a way to inform you about the availability of information provided by Google's NewsApi.

Future add on's:

  • Identifying clickbait articles using Machine Learning and Advance Data Analysis.
  • Identifying which other news sources also talked about the same news article (Great way to identify fake news).
