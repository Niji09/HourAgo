HourAgo Story

How often you have cared about author or source of the news article? Well... I read a ton of news articles and posts online but never found a source which have informed me about the author of news article before reading the article. You have to deep dive into source website without knowing. Sometime they don't even bother to mention author name inside news article.

You can find news article more credible by knowing the source or author of article. HourAgo aims to provide information such as news source, author, publication date and description about news article before you read the article.

HourAgo collect information from Google's NewsApi.org

Why HourAgo?

HourAgo aims to prevent reader from fake news and a great way to do it by providing more information about news article itself.

Future improvements:

  • Identifying clickbait articles using Machine Learning and Advance Data Analysis.
  • Mapping other news source with the related news article (Great way to identify fake news).
